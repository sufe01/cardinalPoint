Introduction

At Cardinal Point Global FZ-LLC ("Cardinal Point"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information in accordance with applicable data protection laws in the United Arab Emirates. By using our website [XCOMPANYURLX] and interacting with our services, you consent to the collection and use of your personal data as outlined in this policy.

What Type of Information We Collect

We may collect various types of personal information from you, including but not limited to:

Contact Information: Such as your name, email address, phone number, and postal address.

Technical Information: Including your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, and other technical data related to your use of the website.

Usage Data: This includes information about how you interact with our website, such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and links clicked.

Communication Data: Records of any communication you have with us, whether through our contact forms, email, or other channels.

How We Collect Information

We collect personal information through the following means:

Direct Interactions: When you voluntarily provide information through our website, such as filling out contact forms or making inquiries.

Automated Technologies: As you navigate through the website, we may automatically collect technical and usage data through cookies, server logs, and other tracking technologies.

Third-Party Sources: In some cases, we may collect information about you from third parties, such as analytics providers or social media platforms.

Why We Collect and Use Your Information

We use your personal information for several purposes, including but not limited to:

Service Delivery: To provide you with the information, products, or services that you request from us.

Improvement of Services: To analyse how visitors use our website and services, allowing us to enhance the functionality and user experience of our offerings.

Marketing and Communications: To inform you about new services, products, promotions, and events that may be of interest to you. You can opt out of receiving such communications at any time.

Legal Compliance: To ensure compliance with any applicable laws and regulations, and to respond to legal requests or enforce our terms of service.

Security of Your Data

We take your privacy and the security of your personal data seriously. To protect your information from unauthorised access, use, or disclosure, Cardinal Point implements a variety of modern security technologies, including SSL encryption for data transmission, firewalls, and secure servers. These safeguards help prevent any unauthorised attempts to access your personal data.

However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Storage Practices

Your personal data is stored securely on servers located in data centres that adhere to industry standards for security and data protection. We apply strict access control measures to ensure that only authorised personnel who require access to your data for legitimate business purposes are allowed to handle it.

To further enhance security, we conduct regular security audits, enforce data protection policies, and maintain organisational measures to minimise the risk of data breaches. In the event of a breach, we will take immediate action to mitigate the impact and notify affected individuals as required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Once your data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymise it in accordance with our data retention policies. If you wish to request the deletion of your personal data, please contact us using the details provided below.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, understand how users interact with the site, and improve overall functionality. These cookies may collect information that can identify you in certain cases, and may be considered personal data under applicable laws.

You have control over your cookie preferences and can manage them through your browser settings or the consent banner provided on the website. For more information, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

Disclosure of Personal Information

We will not sell, rent, or disclose your personal data to third parties except as described in this Privacy Policy or with your consent. We may share your information with:

Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who assist in our operations, such as hosting providers or payment processors, subject to strict data protection agreements.

Legal Authorities: We may disclose your personal data if required by law or in response to a valid legal request.

Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your personal data.
